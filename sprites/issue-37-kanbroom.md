**Species:** Tidy Pokémon

> Kanbroom is a broom-tailed tidy Pokémon that lives inside cluttered project boards. Summoned again when barmo asked Fernie to help clean up the team board at github/projects/24796 (view 2), it drifts row by row across unfamiliar tickets, dusting each one and flagging stale or abandoned cards as candidates to close as "not planned." It never closes anything on its own — it sweeps clutter into a neat proposal and waits for its trainer's nod before any ticket is sealed.
